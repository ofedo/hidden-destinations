Hidden Destinations


Description

This is an AR experience conceived as a guided meditation through the "Alexandru Borza" Botanical Garden of Cluj-Napoca.


Press Start journey to begin. You will be asked to allow access to your location and camera which are needed for this application to work. Don't worry, none of your data is sent anywhere. We don't even serve cookies, sorry!

See the route and locations here. Can't make it to the Garden? That's ok, try out the Simulation instead!
